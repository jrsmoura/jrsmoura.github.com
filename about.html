Sobre Mim

Throughout my training as a physicist, which is a bachelor's degree, a master's degree and a doctorate, I have always worked with numerical simulations in several programming languages, so much that my first simulation, even in 2000, was using BASIC, for a Monte Carlo simulation of a magnetic system, still in under-graduation. Over the years the languages ​​have changed, moving to FORTRAN, then to C / C ++ until you get to the parallelization tools as CUDA and openMP.

I have extensive experience in the construction of numerical simulations, both using Monte Carlo methods and molecular dynamics. I can easily solve complex problems even in tight deadlines.
